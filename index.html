About the shop

Classic barbering, carried forward.

Jacob's Barber Shop combines an established neighborhood feel with the clean, modern atmosphere customers mention in their reviews. The team is personable, professional, and focused on getting each cut right.

Tools and styles change, but patient service, skilled hands, and care for every customer stay the same. That tradition keeps Bellmore families coming back across generations.

  • Consistent cuts and attention to detail
  • Friendly, personable barbers
  • Patient and good with kids
  • Wheelchair-accessible entrance and parking
What we do

Services for every chair.
