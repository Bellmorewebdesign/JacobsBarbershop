About the shop

A local barbershop that gets the details right.

Elegant Barber Shop is a friendly neighborhood shop in Bellmore, known for careful, consistent cuts and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're in for a quick trim or a full fade, Stan and the team take the time to get it right.

We work with men and kids of every age, and we keep the chair comfortable for first-timers and regulars alike. From classic scissor cuts to sharp lineups and hot towel shaves, the goal is simple: you leave happy with the way you look.

  • Careful attention to detail
  • Consistent cuts, every visit
  • Patient and great with kids
  • Welcoming, easygoing atmosphere
